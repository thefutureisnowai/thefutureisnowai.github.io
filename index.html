Mission

   
At Secure Computer Solutions, we prioritize data security and privacy first and foremost. We employ rigorous measures for data safety and cyber security in all our work. We will not collect any customer / user data beyond what is absolutely necessary to conduct business in accordance with the law; although we cannot prevent Google from tracking you: see our privacy policy.

Secure Computer Solutions provides AI, computer repair, and other IT services while promoting ethical and lossless data practices. We believe that it is wrong for any company to gather and use your data for training AI or other purposes, without your explicit permission. We believe data gathering should be an opt-in process, rather than an opt-out process.
