Mission

   
At Secure Computer Solutions, we prioritize data security and privacy first and foremost. We employ rigorous measures for data safety and cyber security in all our work. We will not collect any customer / user data beyond what is absolutely necessary to conduct business in accordance with the law.

Our mission is to provide AI, computer repair, and other IT services while promoting an ethical use of data. We believe that it is wrong for any company to gather and use your data for training AI or other purposes, without your explicit permission. We believe data gathering should be an opt-in process, rather than an opt-out process.
